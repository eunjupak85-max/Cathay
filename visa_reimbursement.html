Crew Visa Reimbursement Policy

For Cabin Crew & Cockpit Crew | Monthly Claim Window: 1st–15th

Eligibility & Attachment Policy

This reimbursement applies to Cabin Crew and Cockpit Crew who pay visa fees related to official duty travel. All claims must include a single document as proof.

  • One document only: Visa fee receipt or approved e‑Visa copy
  • Accepted formats: PDF (preferred), JPG, PNG
  • Submission period: 1st–15th of each month (via SuccessFactors)
